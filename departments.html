Specialities

Medical Departments at a Glance

Each department is staffed by experienced consultants, supported by dedicated nursing teams and modern diagnostic facilities.

❤️

Cardiology

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of heart and vascular diseases using state-of-the-art cardiac catheterisation labs.

  • ECG & Echocardiography
  • Angioplasty & Stenting
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation
  • Heart Failure Management
🧠

Neurology

Expert care for disorders of the brain, spinal cord and nervous system, including stroke and epilepsy management.

  • EEG & EMG Studies
  • Stroke Treatment Unit
  • Memory & Dementia Clinic
  • Headache & Migraine Clinic
🦴

Orthopaedics

Advanced treatment for bone, joint and muscular conditions including joint replacement surgery and sports injuries.

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Arthroscopy
  • Spine Surgery
  • Physiotherapy & Rehab
👶

Paediatrics

Specialised care for infants, children and adolescents in a safe and child-friendly environment.

  • Neonatal ICU (NICU)
  • Vaccination Clinic
  • Developmental Assessment
  • Paediatric Surgery
🦷

Dental

Comprehensive dental services from routine check-ups to advanced oral surgery and cosmetic dentistry.

  • Root Canal Treatment
  • Dental Implants
  • Orthodontics & Braces
  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery
👁️

Ophthalmology

Advanced eye care services including refractive surgery, cataract removal and glaucoma management.

  • LASIK & Refractive Surgery
  • Cataract Surgery
  • Retinal Treatments
  • Glaucoma Management
