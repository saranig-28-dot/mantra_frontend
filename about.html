Our Story

Founded on the Promise of Better Care

LifeCare Health was established with a single purpose: to provide every patient, regardless of background, access to expert medical treatment delivered with warmth and dignity.

From a modest outpatient clinic founded in 2005, we have grown into a full-scale multi-speciality hospital with 12 departments, 120 doctors, and modern diagnostic and surgical facilities.

Today, LifeCare Health serves patients from across the region, combining evidence-based clinical protocols with a genuinely patient-first culture. Our teams in cardiology, neurology, orthopaedics, paediatrics and eight other specialities work together under one roof, supported by an in-house pharmacy, diagnostic laboratory and 24×7 emergency department.
