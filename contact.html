Wellness

Health Tips from Our Doctors

Simple habits that can make a meaningful difference to your health every day.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration supports digestion, circulation and concentration.

🥗

Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains and lean protein in every meal. Limit processed foods and sugar.

🏃

Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity five days a week to improve cardiovascular health.

😴

Quality Sleep

Adults need 7–9 hours of sleep per night. Good sleep strengthens immunity and mental wellbeing.

🧘

Manage Stress

Practise mindfulness, meditation or deep breathing daily. Chronic stress contributes to many serious conditions.

🩺

Regular Check-ups

Don't wait until symptoms appear. Annual health screenings catch problems early when they are most treatable.
